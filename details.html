Thai Green Curry

Cuisine: Thai • Prep 15m • Cook 20m • Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2 tablespoons Thai green curry paste
  • 1 can (13–14 oz) full-fat coconut milk
  • ½ cup vegetable broth (or chicken broth)
  • 1 cup sliced chicken breast (or tofu)
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • Juice of ½ lime
  • Fresh Thai basil leaves
Tip: For deeper flavor, fry the curry paste in the oil until it becomes fragrant and slightly darker before adding coconut milk.
Quick Steps
  • Heat oil; fry paste 1–2 min
  • Add coconut milk; simmer
  • Add protein; cook 5–7 min
  • Add veg; simmer 10–15 min
  • Season & serve

Instructions:

  • Heat oil in a deep skillet or pot over medium heat.
  • Add green curry paste and sauté 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
  • Stir in coconut milk and bring to a gentle simmer.
  • Add chicken (or tofu) and cook 5–7 minutes until nearly done.
  • Add vegetables and broth; simmer 10–12 minutes until tender.
  • Stir in fish sauce, brown sugar, and lime juice.
  • Fold in Thai basil and serve over jasmine rice.
